Income, Automated

Perfect for sending to multiple wallets or diversifying.

Transaction Forwarding

SplitCoin allows you to automate sending Ether to multiple wallets. Perfect for saving for taxes, or forwarding Ether to cold storage.

Automated Diversification

SplitCoin interfaces with Shapeshift to create contracts that convert incoming Ether to other digital currencies. For example, with SplitCoin you can create a contract that converts 25% of incoming Ether to Bitcoin and another 25% to Monero.

Provable shares

SplitCoin allows you to provably share income. This is perfect for miners, selling shares of their income, or developers sharing income from projects.

How it works

Splitcoin creates smart contracts powered by Ethereum!

Alternative Deposit Currencies

Splitcoin interfaces with Shapeshift to accept any currency that Shapeshift can convert to Ether!

Powered by Smart Contracts

You retain control of your Ether at all points, unless you use Shapeshift integrations. You deploy the contract so you own it.

Review Your Contracts

Come back at any time to see the contracts you've deployed

Alternative Output Currencies

Splitcoin can output to Shapeshift addresses. This allows you to receive multiple currencies per deposit!

No Login!

There's no login required to use Splitcoin! Just load the app in a web3 compatible browser.

How to use Splitcoin

Splitcoin is powered by Ethereum. In order to use Splitcion you need to use a browser compatible with Ethereum, or use the Metamask Plugin.
